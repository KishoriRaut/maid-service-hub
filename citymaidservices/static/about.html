Our Story

CityMaid was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize how domestic help is found and managed in Nepal. We recognized the challenges both employers and domestic workers face in finding reliable matches.

Our platform emerged from the need for a secure, transparent, and efficient way to connect domestic workers with employers, ensuring dignity and fair treatment for all parties involved.

Refund Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2024

1. Overview

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds on City Maid Services. We aim to be fair and transparent in our refund process.

2. Contact Unlocking Payments

Payments made to unlock contact information are generally non-refundable. However, refunds may be considered in the following circumstances:

  • The contact information provided is incorrect or inaccessible
  • The profile has been reported and verified as fraudulent
  • Technical issues prevented access to the contact information
  • The profile has been removed from our platform

3. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team within 7 days of the payment
  2. Provide your order ID and reason for the refund
  3. Include any relevant documentation or evidence
  4. Allow up to 5-7 business days for review

4. Refund Eligibility

Refunds will be considered based on:

  • The validity of your claim
  • Our ability to verify the issue
  • Whether the service was actually accessed
  • Any violation of our Terms of Service

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Payments for successfully accessed contact information
  • Payments made more than 7 days ago
  • Cases where the service was used but expectations were not met
  • Violations of our Terms of Service

6. Payment Method

Refunds will be processed through the original payment method used for the transaction. The time taken for the refund to appear in your account may vary depending on your payment provider.
